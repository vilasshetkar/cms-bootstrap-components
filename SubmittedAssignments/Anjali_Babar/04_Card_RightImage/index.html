Lotus

Lotus is India's national flower. The 'Lotus' or water lily, an aquatic plant of the Nymphaea family, has the honour of being India's National Flower.

The large, appealing pink blossoms feature symmetrically arranged petals and broad floating petals that give the impression of serenity and elegance.
