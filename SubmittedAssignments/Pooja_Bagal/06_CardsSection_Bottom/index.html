Discover the ideal blend of comfort and convenience at UNC Charlotte Marriott Hotel & Conference Center. Our new hotel is on the UNC Charlotte main campus, near the University Research and Innovation business parks. Within 10 minutes you can be at The Dubois Center at UNC Charlotte Center City, NoDa, Charlotte Motor Speedway, or PNC Music Pavilion. Hop on the light rail for easy access to Uptown Charlotte, it runs between our hotel and the Charlotte Convention Center. Grab a bit to eat at our Golden Owl Tavern, one of the best local restaurants with outdoor dining and firepits. Our sleek and spacious Charlotte hotel rooms feature Smart TVs with streaming, back-lit mirrors, great views, and lots of natural light. Work out in the hotel fitness center, or relax by our resort-style, outdoor pool. Our Charlotte conference center offers more than 20,000 square feet of luxury event space, scenic outdoor venues, professional planning, and delicious catering services for meetings, groups, and weddings in Charlotte.
