The Amazing Spider-Man

The Amazing Spider-Man is a 2012 American superhero film about Peter Parker, who gains spider-like powers after being bitten by a genetically altered spider.

The Amazing Spider-man 2

The Amazing Spider-Man 2 is a 2014 superhero movie based on the Marvel Comics character Spider-Man.

Spider-Man: No Way Home

Spider-Man: No Way Home is a 2021 American superhero movie directed by Jon Watts and written by Chris McKenna and Erik Sommers.

Spiderman Series

Superhero Film

Spider-Man, also known as Peter Parker, is a Marvel Comics character who has spider-like abilities and uses them to help others. He first appeared in Amazing Fantasy #15 in 1962, created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko as filler for a canceled series.
